Make your Google Analytics stand out with Pimp Analytics. Put any 7 letter isogram in the input boxes below and create your own custom GA snippet. From Wikipedia's article on isograms: "An isogram (also known as a 'nonpattern word') is a logological term for a word or phrase without a repeating letter". Enter non-repeating alphabet [a-z] letters. Note: uppercase letters will be automatically transformed to lowercase. Note: Entering numbers will break your code as per the JS syntax.
